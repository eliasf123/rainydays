Be vary of phishing scams! Usually they occur on email and the fraudster will try to mimic a trusted company. If you click a url from such scams, thay can steal your credit card information and other sensitive information that you type into a form on the page. Use an authenticator like OneLogin if possible on sites you use. Make sure the application you are using are trusted, and check how many weekly downloads it has if you're unsure.
